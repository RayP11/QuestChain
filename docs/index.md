# Truly Local. Always On. Ready for Quests.

**A party of specialized AI agents, running entirely on your machine, working through your tasks while you sleep.**

No cloud. No subscriptions. No data leaving your machine. Ever.

---

## What is QuestChain?

QuestChain is an AI assistant that lives on your computer and actually *does things* — not just chats. It reads and writes your files, searches the web, runs commands, schedules jobs, and works through your task list autonomously in the background.

It runs on small, free, open-weight models through [Ollama](https://ollama.com). No API keys, no monthly bill, no usage limits. Your hardware. Your data. Your agent.

---

## The vision

QuestChain runs a **party of specialized micro-agents** — each one built for a specific job. A researcher. A file keeper. A coder. A scheduler. They each have a name, a class, a set of tools, and a growing history of work.

Instead of one agent trying to do everything, each member of your party stays focused. That focus is what makes small local models productive — a dedicated researcher that only searches and reads sources will outperform a generalist every time.

Build your party. Level them up. Put them to work.

---

## What makes it different

**It works offline.** Disconnect your internet and QuestChain keeps running. The only thing that needs a connection is optional web search.

**It never phones home.** No telemetry. No analytics. No accounts. Optional API keys stay in a local file and go nowhere.

**It works while you sleep.** Drop a task into the quest folder and your agent picks it up automatically — on a timer, in the background, without you watching.

**It grows with you.** Every agent has a name, a class, a level, and a history. The more it works, the more capable it becomes. It's not a tool you reset — it's a companion you build.

**It's open source.** Free to use, fork, and modify. The self-coding loop is already live — QuestChain's own Builder agent has been writing features and pushing commits to the repo.
